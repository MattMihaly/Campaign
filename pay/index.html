ACTAS EA 2023–2026 vs Ambulance Victoria EBA 2024

Experience pays in Victoria.
In the ACT it flatlines.

Victorian paramedics now progress through 12 annual pay increments — every year of experience is recognised on the payslip, all the way to year twelve. Under the ACTAS agreement, an Ambulance Paramedic reaches the top of the scale in about five years. After that, a 15-year veteran and a 5-year paramedic are paid exactly the same salary. Two ladders. One is missing its rungs.

The Ambulance Victoria EBA 2024 introduced annual increments to Year 12 for paramedics, on top of a four-year 16%+ wage deal. The ACTAS structure stops recognising service years early — the only way up is a different job.

Back the claim: real increments in the next ACTAS agreement.

The ACTAS EA 2023–2026 reaches its nominal expiry on 31 March 2026. Bargaining is the moment to fix the structure. Victoria, and other services, have shown it can be done: annual progression that recognises experience to year 12, because retaining experienced paramedics is cheaper than replacing them.

Data sources, methodology & assumptions

What is being compared

Annual rolled-in salaries. ACT figures are Annex A base rates plus the 31.58% operational composite and 2.2% roster adjustment allowance (×1.3378 — this reconciles exactly with the CMTEDD total-salary tables, e.g. AP2.1 $108,457 base → $145,094 total). Victorian figures are annualised aggregated base rates under the AV EBA 2024 multiplied by an estimated rolled-in rate factor for a rotating on-road roster. Both sides therefore include shift penalties on a comparable basis; overtime and superannuation are excluded from both. Note the Victorian RIR varies by roster group — verify the applicable factor in the AVEA 2024 rolled-in rate schedules before quoting figures.

ACT sources

  • ACT Public Sector ACT Ambulance Service Enterprise Agreement 2023–2026, Annex A.
  • Advertised classification ranges, jobs.act.gov.au (Ambulance Paramedic 1: $91,571–$102,237, advertised Feb 2026).
  • CMTEDD Roster Adjustment Allowance salary tables (AP2 and ICP1 increment values by pay-rise date).
  • Intermediate AP1 steps interpolated between the published minimum and maximum; PTS rates are estimates pending confirmation against Annex A.

Victoria sources

  • Ambulance Victoria Enterprise Agreement 2024 (FWC AE528036, approved 14 Feb 2025): 4% p.a. increases for four years; new Year 2, 4, 5, 7, 8 increments; Year 10–12 increments phased in; $12,500 MICA relativity uplift; NEPT classifications moved into the 12-year structure.
  • Victorian Ambulance Union and AEA-V published bargaining outcome summaries.
Verify before you quote. Victorian per-increment figures are working estimates constructed from the agreement's published structure and increases at 21 Oct 2025 rates, and the Victorian rolled-in factor (×1.27 on-road, ×1.22 NEPT) is an estimate. Replace both with the exact values from AVEA 2024 Appendix 1 (aggregated base rates) and the rolled-in rate schedules before publication. ACT PTS figures are also estimates. All rates and loadings live in one RATES object at the top of this page's source.

Assumptions

  • "Years of service" is treated as years in classification for both jurisdictions.
  • 10-year cumulative gap holds today's rates constant (no future EA increases on either side) — a conservative approach.
  • Conversion prices: flat white $5.50; Big Mac $8.20; return economy fare Canberra–Europe $1,900; 60L tank of 91 at $1.85/L; long daycare day $145 (pre-CCS); schooner $9.
